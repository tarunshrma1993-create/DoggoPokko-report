About the Founder

Doggo Pokko is led by Tarun Sharma, who holds an M.Sc. in Chemistry and a Level 2 Dog Trainer certification from Amruth Dogguru, Bengaluru. His lifelong connection with dogs now drives a mission centered on responsible pet parenting, informed training, and meaningful canine awareness worldwide. Tarun is recognized as India's first dog influencer.
